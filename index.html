About Ndakala Advisory & IT Audit

Ndakala Advisory is committed to excellence in IT governance, risk management, and compliance. Our IT audit framework is based on the CISA standard, ensuring that audits focus on:

  • Governance and Management of IT: Assessing organizational IT policies, strategic alignment, and management practices.
  • Information Systems Acquisition and Development: Evaluating the controls and quality in software acquisition, development, and implementation.
  • Information Systems Operations and Support: Reviewing IT service management, incident response, and operational controls.
  • Protection of Information Assets: Verifying controls for data security, privacy, and regulatory compliance.
  • Compliance and Controls Testing: Ensuring adherence to policies, standards, and laws relevant to IT.

Our checklist assists auditors in systematically reviewing these areas, enabling informed decision-making and risk mitigation.

IT Audit Checklist

Our detailed checklist covers all major IT audit areas and provides guidance based on industry best practices:

  • Review of IT governance frameworks and organizational policies
  • Assessment of IT risk management practices
  • Evaluation of software development life cycle and change management
  • Verification of data backup, disaster recovery, and business continuity plans
  • Testing of access controls, user account management, and security monitoring
  • Audit of physical and environmental security controls
  • Compliance verification with legal, regulatory, and contractual IT requirements
